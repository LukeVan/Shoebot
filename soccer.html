Adidas F50 Pro FG On Display

The adidas F50 Pro FG is one of adidas' premier speed cleats for firm ground, built for players who rely on explosive pace and quick acceleration. The lightweight upper uses breathable engineered mesh with a streamlined, low-drag construction. The firm ground stud configuration provides excellent traction on dry grass. Positioned against Nike's Mercurial lineup, the F50 Pro is for players where first step and top speed are the priority. Best for: fast wingers, attacking midfielders, and strikers on natural grass. Key specs: FG studs, speed-focused, adult sizing, firm ground.

Adidas F50 Club FG On Display

The adidas F50 Club FG delivers the F50's speed-oriented design at a more accessible price, making elite performance technology available to a wider range of players. It shares the same low-profile silhouette and speed-forward stud pattern as the Pro, but uses a more durable synthetic upper for regular training sessions. A great option for developing players or weekend warriors who want a fast-feeling cleat without the premium price. Key specs: FG studs, speed-focused, durable synthetic, adult sizing.

Adidas Predator Pro FT FG On Display

The adidas Predator Pro FT FG is the control player's cleat — a precision instrument engineered for midfielders and playmakers who live by touch, accuracy, and ball mastery. The HybridTouch 2.0 upper features Strikeskin fins: raised rubber elements across the striking zone that dramatically increase friction between foot and ball for pinpoint passing and powerful, controlled shooting. A fold-over tongue cleans up the strike zone and provides additional lockdown. The full-length Controlplate 2.0 outsole delivers stability on firm ground while allowing quick pivots and sudden directional changes. One of soccer's most iconic cleat families, and this is the top-tier version. Key specs: HybridTouch 2.0, Strikeskin fins, Controlplate 2.0, FG, adult sizing, control play style.

Adidas Predator Club FG On Display

The adidas Predator Club FG brings the Predator's signature control philosophy to a more accessible price point. The HybridTouch upper and Strikeskin technology are retained, giving players the ball-control feel the Predator is famous for, at a level appropriate for training and recreational leagues. Ideal for players who prioritize touch and control. Key specs: HybridTouch, Strikeskin technology, FG studs, adult sizing.
