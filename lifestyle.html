Nike Dunk Low On Display

The Nike Dunk Low is one of the most recognizable sneakers in the world — originally a basketball shoe in 1985, adopted by skate culture, now a foundational piece of contemporary sneaker culture. Leather upper, Nike Air cushioning, padded collar, and classic Swoosh branding across nearly four decades. Available in an enormous range of colorways — as much a style choice as a footwear decision.

New Balance 9060 On Display

The New Balance 9060 is New Balance's premium dad-shoe-era lifestyle sneaker — a chunky, retro-inspired silhouette with genuine performance credentials. ABZORB and SBS cushioning for a comfortable, cloud-like ride, and a bulky layered aesthetic that has made it a favorite across sneaker culture. One of New Balance's hottest lifestyle models.

Adidas Samba OG On Display

The adidas Samba OG is the shoe that started one of the biggest sneaker trends of the 21st century. Originally designed in 1950 as a soccer training shoe for icy conditions, the Samba's gum rubber sole, T-toe overlay, and suede construction have made it one of the most beloved silhouettes in sneaker history. Its simple, understated design remains unchanged and timeless — and one of the most influential sneakers in the world right now.

Nike Air Max 270 Premium On Display

The Nike Air Max 270 Premium is an elevated version of Nike's lifestyle Air Max, featuring the brand's largest visible Air heel unit. Exceptional all-day comfort from the massive Air unit, with Premium materials and details for a more refined look. A lifestyle shoe that genuinely delivers on comfort — sitting at the intersection of performance technology and street-ready style.

ON Cloud 6 On Display

The ON Cloud 6 is On Running's core lifestyle runner — a shoe that can handle easy runs but is equally at home as an everyday wear. CloudTec pods provide On's signature cushioning underfoot, while the clean, minimal aesthetic makes the Cloud 6 one of the most versatile shoes in On's lineup. Swiss-engineered for runners who want performance DNA in a shoe they can wear all day.
